Club toilet stall

  • Project: Toilet stall - Coronation of Poppea
  • Designed by: Rebecca Howarth
  • Materials: MDF, sourced toilet, shellac, watered-down brown and green acrylic paint, posca markers, sourced stickers

I started by cutting roughly 150 50x50x10mm MDF tiles with a table saw.

The rough, worn-down toilet stall shape was created by smashing blunt tools against MDF sheets until it gave the desired effect; the tiles were then glued on.

After priming with white paint, the toilet stall and sourced toilet were painted with a watered-down green/brown acrylic paint. I used shellac and methylated spirits to separate the paints and give the desired mould effect.

Tags, symbols and anything anyone in the room could think of were then added to the walls and toilet with posca markers and stickers attached.
