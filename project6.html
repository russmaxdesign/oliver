Yara-ma-yha-who sculpture

  • Project: NIDA Sculptural Creatures Project
  • Materials: PLA filament, silicone paint, water-clear resin, plywood

3D printed segments assembled with two-part epoxy resin and filled with Earls multifill.

Water-clear resin mixed with a yellow pigment was poured into a vacuum-formed semi-circle.

Silicone paint mixed with a red oxide pigment was applied with an airbrush in several layers with darker and lighter tones.
