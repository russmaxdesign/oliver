Self-portrait

  • Project: NIDA Self-Portrait Project
  • Details: Inspired by the style of Deb Weir
  • Size: 150cm x 130 cm
  • Materials: Acrylic and markers on canvas

I started by creating a rough self portrait in the artstyle on photoshop, experimenting with different patterns and colours.

I then traced my portrait onto my canvas splashing several layers of colours for the skin and hair.

Then, with a tomato sauce bottle I traced the outlines with thick black and white paint

Finally, I used a black marker to draw the lines along the edges and write the nonsensical words at the bottom of the self portrait.
